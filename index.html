The Evening

What to
Expect

This is our annual thank-you to the retailers, budtenders, and partners we work with every day. Come out, meet some people, eat some food. Leave the badge at home.

01
Food & Drinks
Dinner is set up. Drinks available all night at the cool bar — by the round. Just show up.
02
Live Entertainment
Live DJ, Sterling Scott on stage, and a caricature artist courtesy of Canada's Island Garden. It's a good night.
03
Prizes & Raffle Draws
Industry-sponsored prizes drawn throughout the night, including three grand prizes worth $1,500+ each.
04
Real Connections
One room, one industry, no agenda, no pressure. Just the people you work with every day.
05
LP Showcase
20+ licensed producers set up across the venue — just for retailers and buyers. Walk the floor, sample products, and get some real face time with the brands you carry.
