The Evening

What to
Expect

This is our annual thank-you to the retailers, budtenders, and partners we work with every day. Come out, meet some people, eat some food. Leave the badge at home.

01
Food & Drinks
Dinner and drinks are on us all night. Just show up.
02
Live Entertainment
A DJ, a comedian, and a caricature artist walk into a bar. It's a good night. The caricature station is brought to you by FIGR.
03
Prizes & Raffle Draws
Industry-sponsored prizes drawn throughout the night, including three grand prizes worth $1,500+ each.
04
The People You Work With
150+ retailers, budtenders, and partners in one room. No agenda. Just the industry getting together.
